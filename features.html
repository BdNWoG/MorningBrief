Transforming Information Reception

With information scattered across numerous sources—from social media to news and personal information—Morning Brief tackles the inefficiency in information reception. The current process of finding and consuming information is stuck in an outdated era, requiring unnecessary effort and time.

The Morning Brief Solution

Morning Brief introduces an automatic video assistant designed to deliver personalized morning briefings. By minimizing unnecessary effort, we allow users to focus on what matters most to them. It's like having presidential morning briefs tailored to your personal preferences.

A Real Problem

Studies show that a significant portion of people check their phones shortly after waking up, and information workers spend a large part of their day searching for information. Morning Brief addresses this issue head-on, aiming to save hours of your day by streamlining how you receive information.

How It Works

Morning Brief's service is built on advanced personal data analysis and a personal language model. By analyzing your preferences and presenting the information in a concise video format, we ensure that you get exactly what you need without the fluff.
